Different Types of Clouds

Cumulonimbus Cloud


Typically known as thunder clouds, cumulonimbus clouds are the only type of clouds that can produce, hail ,thunder, and lightening.


Cirrocumulus Cloud


Considered a relatively rare type of cloud, cirrocumulus clouds are commonly known to form during winter or cold weather.


Stratocumulus Cloud

Stratocumulus clouds are the most common type of clouds on Earth. However, they are extremely important to the Earth's energy balance since they reflect solar radiation.
